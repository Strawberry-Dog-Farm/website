Here’s what you can look forward to:

✨ Price: $140

✨ Start Date: Early August through mid September

✨ What’s Included: A fresh, handpicked bouquet every other week—never the same arrangement twice!

✨ 4 bouquets total, spread out over eight weeks—because our bouquets often last longer than 7 days!

✨ Pickup Options: Winter Hill Brewing Company (Somerville, MA) or Strawberry Dog Farm (Oxford, MA)
